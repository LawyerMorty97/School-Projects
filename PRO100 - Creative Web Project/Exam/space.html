In the year 2506 scientists sent up a space station closer to the sun, so it could send energy to the Earth faster. Scientists used the extra energy for the spaceship which sent thousands of people to the new planet Homespace in 2521

Global warming escalated more than suspected, and faster than anticipated. The two poles melted and hundreds of cities got buried underwater. Everyone desperately tried to make a change, but it ended up being all for nothing.

In the year 2521 a new planet with life was discovered, they named it Homespace. Thousands of people were sent into space to make new colonies on the new planet.

A new planet was discovered not long after Homespace. They called it Elyria. They quickly discovered that it were headed for Homespace, and in about 50 years they were going to collide. The scientist knew this before sending thousands of people to Homespace.
